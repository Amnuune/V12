## 5. properties
Object property retrieval and setting performance benchmarks.

| Benchmark | V8 JIT Mean | V12 Baseline Mean | V12 New Mean | V12 Ratio (New vs Base) | V12 New vs V8 JIT |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **`properties/access`** | 1.1709 ms | 12.9430 ms | 2.4152 ms | **5.36x faster** | 2.06x slower |

> [!TIP]
> Prior to implementing shape inference and Polymorphic Inline Caching (PIC), V12 properties access was **11.05x slower** than V8 JIT. With our modifications, the gap has been reduced to just **2.06x slower**, representing a major architectural milestone.
